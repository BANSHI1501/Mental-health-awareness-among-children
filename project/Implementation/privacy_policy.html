Your data is handled with strict confidentiality. We use industry-standard encryption to protect personal information and ensure secure interactions.

MindHorizon provides mental health tools and guidance but is not a replacement for professional medical advice. If you experience severe distress, please seek help from a licensed therapist or medical professional.

We collect minimal user data required to improve our services. Data is never sold or shared without user consent.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their accounts. Any unauthorized access or breach must be reported immediately.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools to enhance the platform. These services adhere to their privacy policies, and we ensure compliance with security standards.

MindHorizon reserves the right to update this policy periodically. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the revised terms.

8. Contact Us

For any inquiries regarding this policy, please contact our support team at support@mindhorizon.com.
