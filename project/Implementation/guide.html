A Fun Guide to Feeling Good & Growing Strong 💪

💡 What is Mental Health?

Mental health is how we think, feel, and act. Just like we take care of our bodies, we need to care for our thoughts and emotions.

🛁 Daily Self-Care Routine

Try these simple steps every day to stay happy and strong!

🌞 Wake Up Happy

Stretch, take a deep breath, and say one good thing about today! 🌅

🍏 Eat Healthy

Good food = good mood! Try eating colorful fruits and veggies. 🍎🥦

🏃 Move & Play

Run, dance, jump, or play outside. Moving makes your brain happy! 🏃‍♂️🎵

💤 Sleep Well

Your brain loves rest! Try to sleep early and wake up feeling fresh. 😴

🎈 Fun Ways to Feel Better

🌞 Sunshine & Fresh Air

Step outside, feel the sun, and take a deep breath! 🍃

🎵 Music & Dance

Play your favorite song and dance like nobody’s watching! 🕺🎶

❤️ Kindness Corner

Kindness makes everyone happy! Try these:

  • Smile at someone today 😊
  • Say “thank you” when someone helps you 🙏
  • Give a hug to a friend or family member 🤗

✨ The Gratitude Jar

Write down things you are grateful for and keep them in a jar. Open it when you need a happy reminder! 🏺

👫 Friendship & Social Skills

Being a good friend helps everyone feel better!

👂 Listen & Share

When your friend talks, listen carefully and show you care.

🙂 Be Kind

Use kind words and help others feel good.
